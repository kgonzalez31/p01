The Start

On a dare, you sneak into a fair that had been abandoned for a couple of years due to all the deaths that occurred in the last couple of months that it was open. You start exploring, hoping to find the power to turn everything on. In the middle of the fair you find a small shack with the words Authorized Personal Only and you pick the lock and go inside. The power switches are inside, and realize that if you turn on the power it might be seen by others causing them to investing what is going on.
